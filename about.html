    About Us

    Introducing Double Pulse Medical Devices LLP a partnership between skilled Engineers with a vision to support Indian Medical manufacturers in their supply chain. Our goal is to reduce reliance on imported wire components and provide Just in Time delivery of essential products such as Stylets, Mandrills, Cassette Rings, and Hypo Tubes. These products are widely used in the Stent manufacturing industry and other medical device manufacturing processes.

    At Double Pulse, we strive to be your reliable partner in the supply chain, ensuring timely delivery of high-quality products that meet your specific requirements. With our expertise and focus on innovation, we aim to strengthen the Indian Medical industry by providing essential components that are locally manufactured, reliable, and of the highest quality.

    Mission

    Our mission at Double Pulse Medical Devices LLP is to empower Indian medical manufacturers by providing superior supply chain solutions. We aim to reduce reliance on imported wire components by offering essential products tailored for the medical manufacturing industry, ensuring timely delivery and exceptional quality.

    Vision

    Our vision is to become a trusted partner for Indian medical manufacturers, renowned for our commitment to quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction. We strive to be at the forefront of the industry, driving innovation and setting new standards for excellence in medical device component manufacturing.
