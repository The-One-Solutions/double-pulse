    About Double Pulse Medical Devices LLP

    Double Pulse Medical Devices LLP, where innovation and expertise come together. Founded by a team of skilled engineers, we're dedicated to helping Indian medical manufacturers with top-notch supply chain solutions. Our goal is to reduce reliance on imported wire components by providing essential products like Stylets, Mandrels, Cassette Rings, and Hypo Tubes promptly. These components are crucial for making Stents and other medical devices. We pride ourselves on quality, using SS 304 V for its strength, elasticity, and straightness, produced on advanced automatic lines. Our mission is rooted in reducing dependence on imported wire components by offering timely delivery of essential products tailored for the medical manufacturing industry.

    At Double Pulse, we specialize in the production of critical components such as Stylets, Mandrels, Cassette Rings, and Hypo Tubes, all of which play a pivotal role in the manufacturing processes of Stents and other medical devices. Our commitment to quality is unwavering, as we utilize SS 304 V—a material renowned for its superior strength, elasticity, and straightness—manufactured on cutting-edge automatic lines.
